About MUHAMMAD UNIVERSITY

Muhammad university of Nigeria(MU) is large private university situated in the ancient commerial city of kano state of Nigeria.

Mission

the mission of Muhammad university is to produce highly skilled and sought after graduates and professionals through innovative research and education, using state-of-the-art technologies.

Vision

The vision of Muhammad university is to be a world-class institution for human capital development, innovation and research in technologies, health science, engineering, arts and social and management science

Programs

At Muhammad university we offer a range of comprehensive and industry-relevent programmes that cater to the diverse interests and career aspiration of our student.

The Admission requirements for undergraduate programs at the Muhammad University, kano are:

1. All admission must be though the Joint Admissions and Mutriculation Board (JAMB)

2. For admission to 100 level courses(Via UTME), candidates must obtain five(5) credit at SSCE in relevant subjects, including credit passes in English and Mathematics, and attain acceptable point in UTME.

3.For admission by Direct Entry (200 level), candidates must have five (5) SSCE credits, obtain at least two (2) A' level passes in relevant subjects or posses ND with credit passes, or posses a good first degree in another field.

4. The University will not accept transfer students until after at least the first two years of its exitence.
